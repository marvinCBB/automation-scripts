Stary Janków [ˈstarɨ ˈjaŋkuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radzymin, within Wołomin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-east of Radzymin, 4 km (2 mi) north of Wołomin, and 24 km (15 mi) north-east of Warsaw.
